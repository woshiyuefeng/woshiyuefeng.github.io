Paper Submission Guidelines:

    Papers should be submitted at
    https://www.easychair.org/conferences/?conf=semibdma2021

    Papers should not exceed 12 pages single spacing in LNCS (Lecture Notes in Computer Science) format. The submission of papers must be in either PDF or Word format. The Authors should exclude the usage of non-English fonts to avoid the problems from reviewers' machines. All papers accepted by SemiBDMA 2021 will be published in a combined volume of Lecture Notes in Computer Science series published by Springer.
